Help decriminalize
alternative schooling
in Kenya

Over the past few months a number of homeschooling families in Kenya have found themselves on the wrong side of the law. The Constitutional Division of the High Court of Kenya has now been petitioned to decide whether Homeschooling is legal or not. Thousands of children not learning in formal schools need your help. Can we count on you?
